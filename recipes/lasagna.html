Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an amazing choice for those who have orange cats.

Myself, I make it everyday for the ones I care about most.

Ingredients

  • 300g dry lasagna
  • 250g tomato sauce
  • Love
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Bring dry pasta to a boil.
  2. Add cheese to water.
  3. Take boiled cheese and drain.
  4. Rinse off pasta. Save pasta water.
  5. Mix tomato sauce with pasta water.
  6. Add love.
  7. Combine ingredients.
  8. Bake at 450 degrees for 450 minutes.
